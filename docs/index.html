🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

Comparing Bosnia & Denmark
🇧🇦 🇩🇰

🌡️ Weather Tomorrow

🇧🇦 Bosnia: 11.1°C ☀️
🇩🇰 Copenhagen: 7.5°C ☀️
🇩🇰 Aalborg: 11.0°C ☀️
🇬🇧 English
Softly falls the night in Bosnia's breeze, Cool at 11, a gentle hush that freezes, No precipitation, calm and clear to see, A peaceful night, for you and me. But look to Denmark's cities, where winds do blow, Copenhagen, Aalborg, their chill does show, Seven and eleven Celsius, with winds quite strong, Yet, calm the skies, with no rain all night long. But if I had to choose, where to be today, In Denmark, Aalborg's gentle wind I'd sway, Warmth like Bosnia's, yet Denmark's gentle breeze, Makes Aalborg the nicest place to be, if you please.
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Lagano pada noć u Bošnjičinom vetru, Hladno na 11, tihi šum koji se zaledi, Ni kap jedne kape, mirno i jasno, Tiha noć, za nas i za vas. Ali pogledajte Danske gradove, gdje vjetar udara, Kopenhagen, Aalborg, njihov hlad dokazuje, Devet i jedanaest Celsius, s vetrom zdravo, Mirisno nebo, bez kiše cijelu noć. Ali ako mi trebaju izabrati, gdje bih bio danas, Danski Aalborg, njegov blagog vjetra mi bi omiljeno mjesto bio, Kako Bošnjičin topli, tako Danski blagog vetra, Aalborg bi bila najljepša lokacija, ako bih mogao izabrati.
