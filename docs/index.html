Comparing Bosnia & Denmark
🇧🇦 🇩🇰

🌡️ Weather Tomorrow

🇧🇦 Bosnia: 8.7°C 🌧️
🇩🇰 Copenhagen: 12.0°C ☀️
🇩🇰 Aalborg: 10.2°C 🌬️
🇬🇧 English
Among the lands where winds do blow, Copenhagen's warmth does gently flow, But Aalborg's gusts will make you sway, While Bosnia's calm begins a peaceful day, Copenhagen's sun shines bright and high, But Aalborg's rain will surely catch the eye, Bosnia's misty veil wraps gently round, Tomorrow in Bosnia would be a peaceful ground.
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Među zemljama gdje vjetar vije, Kopenhagenska toplina ti će blizu biti, Ali Aalborg jejeg vjetar će te natjera, da se vrtiš, Kopenhagensko nebo sjaji visoko u nebo, Ali Aalborgove kiše će sreće očiti, Bosanski mješavin vijena okružuje ti, Sutra bi u Bosni biti mirno mjesto.
