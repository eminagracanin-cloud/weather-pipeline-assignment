Weather Poem

**English:**

Like three sisters dancing in the sky,
Bosnia, Copenhagen, and Aalborg, side by side.
Bosnia's warmth awakens with a gentle breeze,
While Copenhagen shivers in the wind's wild ease.
But Aalborg's calm is like a serene lake,
Reflecting the beauty of a peaceful make.

Tomorrow, I'd choose Bosnia's gentle shore,
Where 11.1°C warmth and clear skies await in store.
No rain to spoil the day, no wind to fright,
Just a gentle haven, a pleasant sight.

**Bosanski:**

Kao tri sestre plešu u nebu,
Bosna, Kopenhagen i Aalborg, pokraj sobne.
Bosanska toplina budi sa blagošću vjetra,
Dok Kopenhagen tresne u vjetru divanom i ljanom.
Ali Aalborgova tišina je kao bezmoran jezerce,
Reflektira lijepoću mirnog života.

Sutra, izabrah Bosnu, tišina i vrelo,
Gdje je 11,1 st. toplina i sunčano nebo.
Ni kiše da prekrši dan, ni vjetar da strši,
Just miran ravan, lijepo mesto.

Note: Please note that the given values are for a specific day and may vary in real-life data.
