Comparing Bosnia & Denmark

🇬🇧 English
The sun shines bright in Bosnia's land A warm twenty-three degrees at hand But wind whispers soft, an easy breeze While Copenhagen waits, in chilly ease The Danish capital, with wind so strong Seventeen degrees, its song Aalborg, a bit cooler still Lies with rain, a gentle will Tomorrow, I'd choose to roam In Bosnia's warmth, and sunny home The day would be a pure delight No clouds in sight, just summer's light
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Sunce sjaji ćisto u bosanskoj zemlji Svaki dan, temperatura dvadeset tri stepena vrijedi Vjetar šumi blago, laganim vjetrom A kopenhagen čeka, hladnim laganjem Danska prijestolnica, s jakim vjetrom Sedamnaest stepena, njena pjesma Aalborg, još hladniji i još Lije s kišom blagim i laganim Sutra, ja bih izabrao Da hodam u bosanskoj vrući dom Dnevnik bio bi svjetlo čisto Nema oblaka na nebu, samo ljetna svjetlost.
