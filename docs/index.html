🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

Comparing Bosnia & Denmark
🇧🇦 🇩🇰

🌡️ Weather Tomorrow

🇧🇦 Bosnia: 22.8°C ☀️
🇩🇰 Copenhagen: 12.2°C 🌬️
🇩🇰 Aalborg: 11.9°C 🌬️
🇬🇧 English
Bosnia's warmth is calling me tonight, A gentle breeze, a peaceful sight, No rain to dampen, no wind to blow, A perfect evening in Bosnia to glow. But Copenhagen's windy roads I'll avoid, A dash of rain, a chill to employ, Not the best choice for me to stray, Copenhagen's weather's blowing me away. And Aalborg's calm, a gentle treat, But a bit of chill, can't be beat, Bosnia's warmth wins, I think I'll stay, In Bosnia's sun, come what may.
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Bosna mjele teškoću veče zove, Mirni vjetar, miran pogled, Ni kiša da smrzne, ni vjetar da teče, Idealan večeri u Bosni da se ogleda. Copenhagenu vjetrenim putovima ću izbjeći, Kiša i hlad koji mi se ne sviđaju, Ovaj odabir ne bi mnogo voleo, Copenhagena vremena me daleko odvode. Aalborgu tiha, a hladna kao lijeva, No i tiha, i hladna kao Bosna, No, Bosna je toplina koja mi se viđa, Bosnoj toploći moram ostati, što bih i mogao.
