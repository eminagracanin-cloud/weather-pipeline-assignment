🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

Comparing Bosnia & Denmark
🇧🇦 🇩🇰

🌡️ Weather Tomorrow

🇧🇦 Bosnia: 11.1°C ☀️
🇩🇰 Copenhagen: 7.5°C ☀️
🇩🇰 Aalborg: 11.0°C ☀️
🇬🇧 English
In distant lands, a tale unfolds, Of Bosnia's calm, where warmth is told, A gentle breeze caresses the ground, With sunshine bright, and not a cloud around. Copenhagen's chill, a northern breeze, A city cold, with icy seas, The city sleeps, in icy hold, But still a beauty, young and old. Aalborg's sea, a gentle sway, A city waking, on a new day, The wind whispers low, of secrets kept, A place of tranquility, in eternal sleep. If I had to choose, where to be, Perhaps I'd wander, wild and free, In Bosnia's fields, where sunflowers stand, And warmth of the earth, beneath my hand.
🇧🇦 Bosanski
U udaljenim krajevima, jedna priča zavjetrila je, O mirnoj Bosni, gdje toplina je pričana, Meku breskva dirkalica uz tlo, S vlažnom svjetlošću, bez jedne oblaka okolo. Kopenhagenov hlad, sjeće sjevernih vjetrova, Grad koji leži, u ledomrljenom grijehu, Grad spava, u hladnom ujetištu, Mada još uvijek lepota, mlad i stari. Aalborgov more, mekuša lopotku, Grad koji se budio, na novi dan, Vjetar šumi tiho, o sakrivenim tajnama, Mjesto mira kojih, u vječnom spavanju. Ako bih morao izabrati, gdje da budem, Možda ja bih se kretao, divlje i slobodno, U bosanskim poljima gdje sunuzi stoje, I toplina cveća za mene, u dlanu. Translated from Bosnian: In distant lands, a tale has unfolded, Of calm Bosnia, where warmth is narrated, A soft breeze caresses the ground, With soft light, without a single cloud around. Kopenhagen's coldness, remembers northern winds, A city that lies, in a thawed sin, The city sleeps, in a cold confinement, Yet still it's beauty, young and old. Aalborg's sea, softens its waves, A city that wakes, on a new day, The wind whispers softly, about hidden secrets, A place of peace that sleeps, in eternal sleep. If I had to choose, where to be, Maybe I would wander, wild and free, In Bosnia's fields, where sunflowers stand, And the warmth of the flowers for me, in my hand.
