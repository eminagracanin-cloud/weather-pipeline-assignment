🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

Comparing Bosnia & Denmark
🇧🇦 🇩🇰

🌡️ Weather Tomorrow

🇧🇦 Bosnia: 20.0°C ☀️
🇩🇰 Copenhagen: 13.2°C ☀️
🇩🇰 Aalborg: 15.1°C 🌧️
🇬🇧 English
In the land of sun-kissed days so bright, Bosnia feels the warmth with gentle might, A temperature of twenty, calm and fair, No clouds to spoil the tranquil air. With Copenhagen's chill, the winds do bite, A temperature of thirteen, not so bright, Precipitation falls, a misty veil, Wishing for a sunnier day to prevail. Aalborg's rain falls hard, a rhythmic beat, Fifteen degrees, a cooler retreat, Precipitation's weight, a somber mood, Not the best of days, but some find it good.
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Dok u Bosni sjajnuju sunčane dane, toplina se oseća blago, mirno, dvaodešet stepeni, hladni vetar, ni mrak koji bi ometao mir. U Kopenhagenu, hladnoća hvata dah, trinaest stepeni, nije tako svetlo, padaju kapljice vode, mračni pokrov, želiš svjetliju dan, bez mraka kov. U Aalborgu, traje teška kiša, petnaest stepeni, hladnija utvrda, težina pada kiša, uzbudljiva atmosfera, ne najbolji dan, ali neki ga volje.
