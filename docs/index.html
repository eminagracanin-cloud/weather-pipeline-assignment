🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

Comparing Bosnia & Denmark
🇧🇦 🇩🇰

🌡️ Weather Tomorrow

🇧🇦 Bosnia: 3.0°C 🌧️
🇩🇰 Copenhagen: 8.4°C 🌧️
🇩🇰 Aalborg: 9.5°C 🌬️
🇬🇧 English
Softly falls the rain in Bosnia's night A gentle breeze at 6.8 knots in sight While Copenhagen shivers in the cold light A windy city at 24.5 knots in flight Aalborg too feels gusty strong and free At 28.8 knots it howls across the sea But if I had to choose a place to be Tomorrow I'd stay in Aalborg with glee Warm air to breathe and minimal rain to spare It's the place to be, without a single care
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Koliko je tiho padaju snijeg lete Mirna vjetrovičica na 6,8 vjetrija metra Dok se Kopnjan drži hladno u noći Zimska gradska vjetrom u letu 24,5 metra Aalborg također osjećaje vjetar slobodan Na 28,8 metara jači, koji zviždice preko mora Ako moram odabrati mjesto gdje bih bio Sutra bih ostao u Aalborgu s veseljem Rastuhu zrak koji se diše i manje kiša da spremi To je mjesto gdje bih, bez zabrinutosti.
