🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

Comparing Bosnia & Denmark
🇧🇦 🇩🇰

🌡️ Weather Tomorrow

🇧🇦 Bosnia: 14.4°C ☀️
🇩🇰 Copenhagen: 11.6°C 🌬️
🇩🇰 Aalborg: 9.5°C 🌧️
🇬🇧 English
Tomorrow, let us choose our place serene, Where temperatures entwine with summer's sheen. In Bosnia's calm, a gentle breeze does stray, A peaceful haven, where warmth meets the day. Copenhagen's winds do howl and whip the air, Cool and brisk, with rain that leaves us bare. Aalborg's gusts are strong, the rain does pour, A somber mood, that we cannot adore. So let us flee the cold and the wet ground, And find our peaceful spot in Bosnia's town, Where sunbeams dance, and warmth does abide, Tomorrow, let us stay in Bosnia's side.
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Buduća, izaberite mesto umereno, Kam temperatura se uklopi s ljetnim sjajem. U bosanskoj miru, svijeće blaga briza, Mirno uporište, gdje toplina sastaje se sa danom. Kopenhagenski vjetar vrije i šepa zraku, Hladnog i vjetar, sa kišom koje nas ostavi golih. Aalborgovi vjetar su jak, kiša teče, Tugava nastrojnost, koju ne mogu voljeti. Pa izbjegavajmo hladnoće i blato, I pronađimo naš mirovni mjesto u bosanskom gradu, Gde sunčice igraju, i topla toplina stoji, Buduća, izaberi s nama naš bosanski kraj.
