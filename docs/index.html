🇬🇧 English
Bosnia shines bright under sun's warm light, 22 degrees, a day so right. Copenhagen's winds blow strong and cold, While Aalborg's rain makes its heart unfold. But tomorrow, where would you rather be? Aalborg's warmth and sunshine await thee.
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Bosna sjaja pod svjetlo sunca, 22 stepen, dan tako tko. Kopenhagenu vjetar duri i hladno, Aalborgu kiša otvara njegovo srce. Ali sutra, gdje bi voljeli biti? Aalborgu toplina i sunce čekaju te.
