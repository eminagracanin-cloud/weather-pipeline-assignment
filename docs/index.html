Bosnia shines bright with sun-kissed days, No clouds to hide the radiant rays, Copenhagen's gentle breeze whispers low, Aalborg's misty veil begins to show. But where to go tomorrow's tale we'll tell, If warmth and calm are what your heart would sell, Then Bosnia awaits with open arms so free, A perfect place to be, wild and carefree.
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Bosna sija i danima ojasnjava, Nitko ti nije ni mrak za zasjenjivanje, Kopenhagen hladan šum digne niske, Aalborga magla začinila se počinje pokazivati. Ali gdje ide priča o sutrašnjem dnevima, Ako su toplina i tiška stvar tvoje srce htjela. Onda Bosna očekuje s otvorenim rukama, Iskrena mjesto da si, divlje slobodni.
