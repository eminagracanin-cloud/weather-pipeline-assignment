🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

🇧🇦 Bosnia    🇩🇰 Denmark
**English Section** A gentle breeze whispers through the lands, Bosnia, Copenhagen, Aalborg, each a gentle hand. The temperature's calm, a soothing sight, But which would be most pleasant tomorrow, a quiet delight? Bosnia's warmth beckons, 11.1°C a gentle charm, Copenhagen's chill, a mere 7.5°C, a colder alarm. Aalborg's temperature, 11.0°C, a soothing breeze, But wind and breeze, which one would bring us to our knees? The answer lies not in the wind's gentle sway, But in a place where calm and warmth entwine the day. Tomorrow, we'd rather be in Aalborg's gentle grasp, Where 11.0°C and a soft breeze would bring a pleasant pass. **Bosnian Sekcija** Neobično tihi vjetar prolazi kroz zemlje, Bosna, Kopenhagen, Aalborg, svaki nešto drugačije. Temperatura mirisava, pogled kojeg mi čini hladnim, Ali gdje bi bilo najljepše biti sutra, bez straha ili straha? Ljubav Bosne je snažna, 11.1°C, lako čakavski čaj, Kopenhagen je hladniji, 7.5°C, hladan dan bez jutra. Aalborg je tih, 11.0°C, vjetar koji umiruje, Ali kome će biti ljubavni ujutru bez straha ili straha? Odgovor nije u tihi vjetar koji prolazi šumom, Već u mjestu gdje tiha temperatura ljubav čini sjajnom. Sutra bismo išle u Aalborg, s 11.0°C i vjetrom koji umiruje, Iako bi Bosna i Kopenhagen također bili lijepi mjesti.
