🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

🇧🇦 Bosnia    🇩🇰 Denmark

🇬🇧 English

Like three sisters dancing in the sky, Bosnia, Copenhagen, and Aalborg, side by side. Bosnia's warmth awakens with a gentle breeze, While Copenhagen shivers in the wind's wild ease. But Aalborg's calm is like a serene lake, Reflecting the beauty of a peaceful make. Tomorrow, I'd choose Bosnia's gentle shore, Where warmth and clear skies await in store. No rain to spoil the day, no wind to fright, Just a gentle haven, a pleasant sight.

🇧🇦 Bosanski

Kao tri sestre plešu u nebu, Bosna, Kopenhagen i Aalborg, pokraj sobne. Bosanska toplina budi sa blagošću vjetra, Dok Kopenhagen trese u vjetru divanom i ljanom. Ali Aalborgova tišina je kao bezmoran jezerce, Reflektira ljepotu mirnog života. Sutra, izabrah Bosnu, tišina i vrelo, Gdje toplina i sunčano nebo. Ni kiša da prekriši dan, ni vjetar da strši, Just miran ravan, lijepo mjesto.
