🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

Comparing Bosnia & Denmark
🇧🇦 🇩🇰

🌡️ Weather Tomorrow

🇧🇦 Bosnia: 18.1°C 🌧️
🇩🇰 Copenhagen: 10.0°C 🌬️
🇩🇰 Aalborg: 8.1°C 🌧️
🇬🇧 English
Softly falls the rain in Bosnia's land, Eighteen degrees, a gentle hand, Breezy but calm, the winds do say, Nineteen millimeters fall by day. Copenhagen's chill begins to creep, Ten degrees cold, a windy leap, Twenty-nine kilometers an hour strong, Just point two millimeters all day long. Aalborg's misty veil enfolds, Eight degrees and cool to hold, Twenty-five kilometers an hour so fast, Ten point one millimeters at last. So let us go to Bosnia's shore, Tomorrow's warmth we ask for once more, No more Aalborg's chill, no wind at all, In Bosnia, we dance through it all.
🇧🇦 Bosanski
Slatko padaju oblake u bosanskoj zemlji, Dvadeset osam stepeni, blagu rukom, Zelenutav i miran, vetar kaže, Devetnaest milimetara pada za дан. Kopenhagenova hladnost počinje da prodira, Dasje stepeni hladnog, velikom udarom, Dvacetdevet kilometara često snažan, samo dvanaest milimetara dana dug. Aalborgova mraljena zavera se zauzdela, Sedamnaest stepeni hladno i hladno, Dvadesetpet kilometara tako brzo, Dokaz jedanaestna milimetara na kraju. Dakle, pođimo na bosansku obalu, Utomorovo sušto zamoljavamo, Ni Aalborgova hlad, ni vetar nema, U Bosni, živimo sve.
