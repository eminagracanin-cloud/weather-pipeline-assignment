Aalborg's chill, a coastal breeze, Eight degrees, a windy tease, A sprinkle falls, a fleeting rain, Three millimeters, a light refrain, Thirty-two knots, a gale's wild sway, A city's heartbeat, in a windy day. But if I had to choose, where to be, Tomorrow I'd choose Copenhagen, happy to be, Warm and dry, with a city's delight, Copenhagen's spring, a lovely sight.
