🌦️ Weather Poem 🌦️

🇧🇦 Bosnia    🇩🇰 Denmark
**English Section: Whispering Skies** In Bosnia's gentle breeze, 11.1 degrees high, A warmth that whispers secrets to the open sky. Copenhagen's chill, 7.5 degrees so fine, A Danish charm that beckons, icy wind divine. Aalborg's windswept streets, 11.0 degrees serene, Aalborg's beauty stirs, a soothing, peaceful sheen. Their skies are clear, a transparent canvas wide, No clouds to hide the sun, no rain to abide. Which place to choose for tomorrow's delight? Perhaps Bosnia's warmth, where stars shine bright. But if you yearn for something fresh and clean, Aalborg's gentle breeze may be the perfect scene. **Bosanski Odjek: Šumovi Nebeski** U Bosni, blagu zimi, 11.1 stepena visoko, Tople zvukovi šumaju otvorima za nebo. Kopenhagen, hladna temperatura, 7.5 stepena lagano, Danska čarolija poziva, hladnim vjetrom. Aalborg, širokog dijela, 11.0 stepena mirno, Aalborgova ljepota stvarna, uspoka, tišina i svetlost. Njihova nebela, transparentni štand, širok, Ni oblaci da zasaka suncu, ni kiša da bude prisutna. Koji mjesto izabrati za sutrašnje rado? Možda Bosna, toplo i toplom, gdje zvijezde svijetle. Ali ako si zaljubljen i u štoča čisto, Aalborgova lagana bura, savršeno mjesto.
