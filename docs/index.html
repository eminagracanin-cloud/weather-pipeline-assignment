Comparing Bosnia & Denmark
🇧🇦 🇩🇰

🌡️ Weather Tomorrow

🇧🇦 Bosnia: 4.8°C 🌧️
🇩🇰 Copenhagen: 9.8°C ☀️

🇬🇧 English
Rain falls in Bosnia's chill, A gentle breeze that soothes the still, Copenhagen's calm without a drop, Aalborg's gusts that leave the heart on thehop. But if I could choose a place to be, Tomorrow, I would wander to Denmark's sea.
